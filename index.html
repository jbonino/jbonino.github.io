About

Zombie panic is a roblox game where humanity fights against a deadly zombie virus and its creatures, which was caused by the negligence of Xen company. Players have 2 different game modes, where they're separated by Human and Zombie teams. Fight for each side in Deathmatch and Infection modes, buy new equipment, characters, and other features to progress in the game and have more advanced experience. Be in the top player's leaderboard and more!

Which side do you want to fight for? Be a survivor or an adherent of Xen company, it's up to you, join the game and try yourself!
